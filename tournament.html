Instructions

  1. Create an account to register. If you already have an account, skip to the next step.
  2. Log-in to your account.
  3. Begin registration by clicking HERE or by clicking on "Account Panel" at the top on the right sidebar.
  4. To register for your individual events*, click on "Register Individual Events" and follow the instructions.
    5. - If you are part of a groupset for Team Competition, make sure you hit the event checkbox for the "Group Set Event" as you select all your other individual events.
    - Once you receive email confirmation, you should be able to go to "My Events" in your "Account Panel" to view the events you signed up for.
  5. If you are in a groupset, you must also then click on "Register for Team Competition" from your Account Panel.
    6. - If you are team captain, you must create your team. Your other group members can then join your team by pulling it up in the dropdown menu.
    - You will not be able to create or join a team if you did not checkmark the "Group Set" event when you were registering for your individual events.
